About us

Gojek is Southeast Asia's leading technology group and a pioneer of the integrated super app model. Gojek was founded in Indonesia with a mission to improve lives through technology and unlock more inclusive growth in the region. With a presence in more than 200 cities, we provide access to more than 20 on-demand services, including transportation, payments, food delivery, and more. As of March 2020, Gojek’s application and its ecosystem have been downloaded over 170 million times by users across the region.


In Vietnam, the super app connects more than 150,000 driver-partners and 80,000 food merchants to millions of Vietnamese consumers. We currently offer services for motorbike ride-hailing (GoRide), food delivery (GoFood), and courier (GoSend) – and we’re working on the rest!

The Story of Gojek

Gojek was founded in 2010 as a call centre to optimize the service of motorcycle taxis (ojeks) in Indonesia. Ojeks, till today, form a crucial part of the country's informal economy. We started with a fleet of only 20 motorcycle taxis, along with the belief that these drivers could do more than just transport people, if they are part of a system that enables trust and transparency.


With the principle of using technology to improve the lives of users, the Gojek app was launched in Indonesia in January 2015, offering motorbike ride-hailing (GoRide), courier (GoSend), and personal shopping (GoMart) services. Today, Gojek has expanded our mission of removing life’s daily issues and transformed into a super app: a one-stop platform with more than 20 services, connecting over 170 million users across the region with over 2 million driver-partners and 500,000 GoFood merchants.


With the insights and experiences gained from being the pioneer and leader in Indonesia, we have successfully scaled our business across five countries in Southeast Asia.


Gojek began its services in Vietnam in 2018, under the brand GoViet. This hyper-local approach helped us gain a deep understanding of the unique needs of the Vietnamese. Since 2020, GoViet has unified with the bigger Gojek umbrella so we can more effectively innovate at scale and create more integrated solutions to life's everyday issues.


Gojek is dedicated to solving the daily challenges faced by consumers, while improving the quality of life for millions of people across Southeast Asia – especially those in the informal sector and micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs).
